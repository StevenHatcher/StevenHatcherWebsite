Who I am:

Hey! My name is Steven Hatcher this is my website! I live in Regina, SK. I was born and raised here, always keeping busy with sports, technology, and taking things apart to learn how they work (sorry mom).
I am currently finishing my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science at the University of Regina (Fall 2025)! If you've found this page, I'd be an excellent candidate to hire!

I like to maintain a balance between staying active and enjoying the quieter times in life!

What I do:

Technology

Let's get the obvious one out of the way; I love technology. I love designing and configuring hardware to turn code into something tangible. I love structuring and creating software. I love combining the two so I can bring my ideas to life. If you want to learn more about what I've made, take a look at my portfolio and my Github!

Rugby

Regina Condors RFC

I started playing rugby in 2024 and can't even begin to tell you how life-changing it has been. I signed on with no prior contact-sport experience and while it took me some time to find my footing, I got it eventually thanks the undying support of my teammates. In what was originally just a way to get some cardio back into my routine following the pandemic, I ended up meeting the most passionate and supportive people I have ever met, and found myself growing as a person more than as an athlete.

Join the team! We're always happy to have new players join the team and help expand the sport within the city!
